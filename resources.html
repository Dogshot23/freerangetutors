A note on these materials

Everything here was made because I needed it and couldn't find a decent free version elsewhere. Most started as one-offs for a specific class — tidied up and shared here when they proved useful enough to keep using.

They're not perfect. Some have rough edges. The teacher notes on each card are honest about what works well and what doesn't. If you adapt something and the adapted version is better, that's a good outcome — that's what they're here for.

No licence text to worry about. Use them freely, adapt them, print them, give them to other teachers. The only thing that would feel strange is claiming to have made them.
