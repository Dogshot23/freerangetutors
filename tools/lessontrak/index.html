How to use this in class

Fill in the log immediately after a lesson — even two or three lines is enough. The goal is to capture what actually happened, not what you planned. What did you cover? What didn't land? What needs revisiting next time?

It works best as a quick end-of-session habit rather than a detailed record. The entries are searchable, so you can look back across a student or group over several weeks without hunting through a notebook.

I use it on my phone immediately after the lesson ends, before I lose the thread. Filling it in the next morning is better than nothing but you lose the honest detail.
