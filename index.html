Why this exists

After enough years teaching, the weight of repetitive admin, mediocre platform tools, and bloated edtech systems becomes genuinely exhausting. A lot of what passes for "teaching technology" adds friction rather than removing it. The lesson plan templates are too long. The trackers require too much maintenance. The platforms cost too much and do too little of what you actually need.

Free Range Tutors is an attempt to build something different. Small tools. Specific resources. Things that solve one problem well and stay out of the way. The goal is lower mental fatigue, not higher productivity metrics.

Everything here was built because I needed it and couldn't find a decent free version. Most of it is still evolving. Some of it is experimental. All of it is made for teaching real students in real lessons.
